FIRST

FIRST Robotics Competition is a high school level robotics competition where teams work to build 120 pound robots over the course of 6 weeks. These teams then go on to compete against each other in local and international competitions. Learn more about this year's competition here:

According to the FIRST website, “the mission of FIRST is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.” FIRST teams value gracious professionalism, coopertition, and innovation as we strive to encourage the foundation of “more than robots."
